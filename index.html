Must-Visit Places

Prithvi Cafe

I had visited in December 2018

Quaint little restuarant tucked inside a by-lane of Juhu. Blink and you will miss the entrance to the cafe. So be alert enough to spot the black signboard that says "Prithvi Cafe" while you are on the main road. The restaurant has a reasonably good menu at reasonable prices. The food is delicious, the servings are quite large and some dishes can be shared by 2 people. It attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebritites on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid disspointments. On other days, the crowd there is pretty happy. hosts the famous "Prithvi Theater Fest", which you must attend if possible.

Review On The Movies

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

I watched it today and I loved it. You should have a minimal previous understanding of the story, or you will miss 40% of the fun. You should watch Infinity War, Civil War, Black Panther, Age of Ultron, Ant-Man and the Wasp before watching Avengers: Endgame. The entire movie pays homage to most of the other Marvel superhero movies. You will enjoy it a lot. There is, at the beginning of the movie, a (spoiler alert) head dismemberment that is quite impressive. The movie is based on the most-talked action-packed Sci-Fi series. The movie shows how the Avengers Assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe. THe movie is lengthy, and it does run for 3 hours all worthwhile.

Lion King
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

The "Lion King" is ab impressive movie, with outstanding visuals and a story that is true to the original. The voice acting is good, with James Earl Jones reprising Mufasa. Despite criticism, the movie is true to animal nature and has funny moments for children to enjoy. It is worth seeing for children, as the original film was a childhood favorite.
